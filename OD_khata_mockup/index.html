How it works

Self-host in an evening. Yours forever.

Spin it up

One Docker command on any box you control — a Pi, a VPS, the NAS in the cupboard. No account, no cloud, no telemetry.

Create your plans

Asset, chit, loan, 401(k) or holding. The wizard adapts to each type and writes a plain-language summary to sanity-check.

Log it as it happens

Snap the UPI screenshot, tag the funding source, let roll-forward do the arithmetic. Net position updates the moment you save.
