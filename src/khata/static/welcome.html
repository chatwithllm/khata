02

Your ledger lives in your house.

A khata was never a service. It was a book you kept, bound in cloth, that no one else could read. This one is the same — just faster at arithmetic.

¶1

Self-hosted, single file

One SQLite file on hardware you own. Back it up by copying it. No cloud, no account with us — there is no “us” server.

¶2

Multi-user, on your terms

Family members sign in locally or with Google — to your instance. Shared plans carry contributor attribution and ownership shares.

¶3

Money math you can audit

Integer minor units, banker-grade rounding, balances derived from entries — never stored, never drifting. The test suite is part of the product.

₹ and $ · one book · your rate

Genuinely multi-currency

Hold gold in rupees and a 401(k) in dollars. The dashboard converts everything to your base currency at rates you set — and tells you plainly when it can't.

Two worlds, one family

Built for households that live across countries — where a chit in Hyderabad and a paycheck in Houston belong on the same page.
